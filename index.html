Tiny Productions

Capturing candid and natural moments is the essence of my photography. Before the photoshoot I like to meet or get to know my clients a bit more, so when the day comes, we are all at ease with each other, and comfortable in our presence and in front of the camera. I am a very open, approachable person, and I tend to chat a lot during the photoshoot. On my photo walks, we – walk, and talk, and as we go, I take photos when the moment is right. Of course, I will direct occasionally and give instructions, but my final goal will always be capturing the moments when you are at your most relaxed, natural self.

Ljerka Kukurin

My name is Ljerka Kukurin and I am a 35 year old photographer from Rijeka, Croatia. After spending 3 years in Tromsø, I have moved to Stavanger and absolutely fell in love with Jæren where I hope to stay for a long time to come. I have been a photographer for almost 10 years now, and in the year 2022 I have decided to finally start a company of my own, something that will embody and represent both me, and all the things I love. Taking portraits and connecting with people, deeply fullfills me. I like to say I love all things tiny and beautiful, which ultimately brought me to naming my company Tiny Productions.
